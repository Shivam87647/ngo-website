Why Children?

Across India, millions of children face daily challenges just to survive—access to education, proper nutrition, healthcare, and a safe environment remain out of reach for many. Poverty, child labor, and early marriage steal their childhood and potential. But together, we can change this.

At Goonj, we believe every child deserves a life full of opportunities, dignity, and hope. Through innovative initiatives and community-driven action, we work to ensure children not only survive but thrive. Your support can help build brighter futures for these young lives.

Who We Are

Goonj is a social enterprise dedicated to turning urban surplus into rural resources, addressing critical needs of underprivileged communities, especially children. Since our inception, we have impacted millions across India by focusing on long-term, sustainable change.

We work closely with communities, educators, and local authorities to address children’s needs—from education and clothing to healthcare. By transforming everyday urban resources into meaningful support, we empower children, families, and communities to thrive.
