Our Vision

Our vision is to create a compassionate and inclusive society where every individual, regardless of background, has access to quality education, basic healthcare, and equal opportunities. We aim to empower communities, inspire positive change, and build a sustainable future filled with dignity, hope, and social responsibility for all.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help underprivileged people by providing education, basic healthcare, and essential support, so they can live a better life with dignity and hope.

Environmental Awareness

We promote environmental awareness by encouraging tree plantation, waste reduction, cleanliness drives, and sustainable practices to protect nature.

Women Empowerment

We promote women empowerment by providing education, skill development, and equal opportunities, enabling women to become confident and independent.

Child Welfare

We focus on child welfare by ensuring access to education, nutrition, healthcare, and a safe environment, helping children grow with dignity and care.

Over 4 decades of ensuring happier childhoods

In 1979, Rippan Kapur – an Indian Airlines purser – started CRY with 6 friends and just Rs. 50. Today, over 4 decades later, CRY works with 144 project partners across 20 states in India and has impacted the lives of over 4.7 million children.
